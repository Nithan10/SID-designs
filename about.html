Our Story

How it all started

It all started when college friends, Nithish and Sarvesh, set upon their journeys to design their dream homes. It didn’t take them long to realise how fragmented the industry was. Factors like identifying the right professionals, coordination of tasks, and ensuring fair price and quality were pain points faced by many.

Determined to fix this and offer a happier experience to homeowners, they built a much needed bridge for the industry. SID Designs was founded in 2014. It is now the trusted brand for complete home interior design and renovation for thousands of homeowners.

Milestones

Our Growth Journey

From a dorm room idea to an industry leader.

2014
The Beginning

Founded by Nithish & Sarvesh to disrupt the fragmented interiors industry. Operations launched in Erode.

2015
Rapid Expansion

Expanded to Goa, Chennai, and Coimbatore. Recognized as ‘Innovative Start-up of the Year’.

2017
Global Recognition

Awarded ‘World’s Most Innovative Company’ by Fast Company. Milestone crossed: 1,000+ Homes Designed.

2019
Going International

Launched operations in Singapore. Conferred ‘India’s most admired home interiors brand’.

2022
Unicorn Status

SID Designs achieved Unicorn status! The dream became a reality, valuing the company over $1 Billion.

2024
Decade of Excellence

Celebrating 10 years! Inaugurated India's largest Experience Centre in Delhi and crossed 50,000+ happy homes.
